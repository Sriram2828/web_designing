Grandma's Secret Apple Pie

The apple pay recipe has been in my family for generations and never fails to delight

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup of sugar
  • 2 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 6 medium-sized Apple, sliced and peeled

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375oF (190oC)
  2. In a bowl, mix the sugar and cinnamon
  3. Toss the Apple in the sugar cinnamon mixture
